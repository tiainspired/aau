How to Make a Cappuccino

A simple 8-step guide

Steps About

Introduction

Cappuccino is one of the most popular coffee drinks in the world, made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Follow these easy steps to prepare a café-style cappuccino at home.

Steps

  1. Brew a shot (1–2 oz) of espresso.
  2. Pour cold milk into a pitcher.
  3. Steam the milk using a steam wand until it doubles in volume and becomes velvety.
  4. Hold back the foam with a spoon and pour the steamed milk into the espresso.
  5. Spoon the milk foam on top of the drink.
  6. Optionally, sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon on top.
  7. Serve immediately in a small cup (5–6 oz).
  8. Enjoy your homemade cappuccino!
