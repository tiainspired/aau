Step-by-Step Guide

  1. 1. Gather all your ingredients — beans, milk, sugar, and a clean cup.
  2. 2. Preheat your espresso machine and ensure it’s clean.
  3. 3. Grind fresh coffee beans to a fine consistency.
  4. 4. Brew the espresso shot and pour it into a cup.
  5. 5. Steam and froth the milk until it’s creamy and velvety.
  6. 6. Pour the milk over the espresso carefully.
  7. 7. Add a dusting of cocoa or cinnamon for flavor.
  8. 8. Serve immediately and enjoy.
