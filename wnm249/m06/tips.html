Expert Tips

  • ☕ Use freshly roasted coffee beans for the best aroma.
  • 🥛 Always use cold milk for better frothing results.
  • 💨 Clean the steam wand after every use to prevent residue.
  • ⚖️ Balance espresso and milk in a 1:1 ratio for true cappuccino taste.
  • ❤️ Practice your milk pouring technique for latte art mastery.
